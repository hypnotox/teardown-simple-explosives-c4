A mod adding C4 explosives.

[h2]Features[/h2]

- Unlimited C4s
- High detail model

[h2]Usage[/h2]

When you have the tool selected simply left click to place a C4.
To explode all planted C4s, simply have the tool selected and right click while not in range to grab anything.
